About Me

Name: Carrie Song

Home: I was born and raised in Shanghai, China. I lived in D.C. for about 3 years, and I currently live in Seattle, Washington.

Career Path: I graduated from George Washington University in 2015 with a Master of Accounting degree. After graduation, I worked in public accounting for a couple of years, and then went to a IT consulting firm as a business management analyst. Being in the IT industry made me realize my interest in tech and that coding may be an essential skill in the future. Therefore, I started learning coding at the UW coding bootcamp.

Hobbies: Hiking; Baking; Playing Guitar; Cuddling with my cat Teddy.

Favorite Movie & TV Shows: The Office; The Shawshank Redemption.
